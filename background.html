The Internet was first publicly introduced in 1991 through World Wide Web by Tim-Berners Lee. But it was actually invented in 1983 by ARPANET. ARPANET stands for Advanced Research Projects Agency Network. It was established in 1960 President Eishenhower. Following the Internet's invention came a bunch of websites were invented. Yahoo was the first among these websites. It was launched in 1995 by Jerry Yang. Then Google followed in 1998. Amazon - the largest internet based sales site was founded by Jeff Bezos. In the 2000's websites like Youtube and social media sites such as MySpace and Facebook were launched. These inventions improved and made communication easier across the world.

The idea of Internet was first proposed by J.C.R. Licklider. Licklider was a porfessor at Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He later joined the DARPA-Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency- in 1962. The basis for internet connections was made when Leonard Kleinrock invented packet switching. Packet switching is the new switching method. The original method is called circuit switching. Packet switching divides the network communication into separate packets and are sent to their destination location which are reassembled together. Each packet is about one Kilobyte length.
